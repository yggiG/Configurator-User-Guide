Configurator Environment User Guide

Configurator

This is the first view of the configurator. Go through the next steps to learn about more about it.

Step Container

Here you will see the title of the step in which you currently are and the available options.

Navigation Buttons

To navigate through the configurator steps, click the left or right arrow buttons.

Product view

Here you will see the result of the chosen options.

Summary button

To show a summary of the chosen options, click the summary button on the left top corner.

Summary view

A summary of the step, corresponding chosen option and its price.

Product button

To return again to the Product View, click the Product button on the left top corner.

More information button

For more information about the product/option, click the round button on the right top corner of the option.

More information View

This is the modal with more information about the product/option.

Exit More information View

To close the More information View, just click the Close button or click anywhere on the darker area around the modal.

Amount

To change the amount of product that you want to order, use the up or down arrows.

Print summary button

To print the summary of the order you are about to make, click the Print summary button.

Submit button

If you are done, click the Submit button on the right bottom corner to submit the order.
